Coil head

Coil-Heads, also known as Spring Men, are tall, humanoid entities. They are eyeless mannequins with agape mouths, no forearms and a flexible spring for a neck. Their bodies are punctured with nails that are surrounded by what seem to be stains of blood. Coil-Heads are difficult to counter when alone, more especially if you're dealing with another harmful entity at the same time.

Thumper

Thumpers, colloquially known as Crawlers, are two-legged, highly aggressive entities. Thumpers are a carnivorous species of the order Chondrichthyes. Their name comes from the fact that Their arms are very strong, and they occasionally use them to stomp prey. They can reach great speeds in a straight line.

Bracken

Brackens, also known as Flower Men, are shadowy, humanoid entities. They are a bipedal vertebrate with skin the color and texture of a red beet. The name is coined for what appear to be leaves protruding from its upper spine. They are vicious predators capable of treading in near silence, until its too late.
